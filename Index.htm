Welcome to Color NE-Thing

Objective

  A coloring book phone app that allows you to color any picture you want. There's not an app that allows the user to choose any selection of picture and color it. Also, the app will allow you to take a picture and render that picture to coloring book format and color it how you want. There are other coloring book apps, but they only allow the user to choose from a group of provided pictures or a specific genre. Our solution is better than what is out because no app allows a user to take a picture and then render it on spot to color. Our solution also lets the user choose from multiple types of pictures to choose from

Proposed Milestones

  We had several steps to complete our milestones. First, is to get the picture outline rendering working efficiently, and then incorporating the search bar to find pictures. Lastly, linking the app to the camera and photo album to use pictures the user has stored on their phone or will currently take.

Testing

  For our testing we let a group of children play with our app. They are 8, 10,and 13 years old. They confirmed it was simple and fun. Although depending on the age we found that it was harder for young children to find pictures on the web. Their mother, on the side, while the children were playing cursed us because now her children were going to be asking to play with her phones.
